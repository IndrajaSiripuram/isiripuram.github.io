OVERVIEW

Musician For you is a Musician booking app to not just make it easy for the users to book a musician but also to encourage local musicians and their talent. This app has a list of all the local Musician Bands to choose from depending on the occasion. The app also has a preview option where users can view the Bands recent performances and decide if the band really meets their expectations and then proceed to book. The App also provides users with a chat feature where users can directly chat with the Band and clarify their doubts and list about their specifications. Through thorough research and testing, the website was designed by prioritizing the needs and preferences of the users.

The Problem

People find it difficult to find a Musician who fits in their budget, even if they find the Musicians after spending lot of time they demand for the transportation which is not included in the payment. People also have trust issues till the musicians come to the event. Also the local Musicians don’t get any customers because they aren’t popular.

The Solution

Musician Booking app will let users find a best local musician who meet all their expectations and the requirements. The App also has an option to filter depending on the users needs which saves the time. The App helps the local musicians to showcase their talent and get customers to prove their talent.

USER RESEARCH SUMMERY

Study type: Unmoderated study

Number of participants: 5

Location: Remote

Duration: 15 min

PAIN POINTS

Time Taking

Without knowing where to start and with no musician contacts it is difficult and time taking to find and book one.

Meet expectations

After contacting a musician after lot of research and then get to know he is not available on the day we need.

Trust

Even after paying the advance we can’t trust if they show up because of all the fake accounts on Social media.

Budget

Finding a musician who fits in our budget is very difficult. Non local Musicians also demand for the transportation in addition.

USER JOURNEY MAP

The goal of the user was to book a musician for their daughter’s wedding but didn’t know where to start. Searching for one who meets all the requirements is a time taking process. The user wants to book a good musician who fits in their budget in less time.

PAPER WIREFRAMES

The main goal of the app was to make it easy for the users to book a musician as easily as possible and with no confusion.

The wireframes were designed by sketching some quick alternatives to each screen and the best options were marked and are put together in a single screen and formed a wireframe.

DIGITAL WIREFRAMES

In order to address most of the users pain points we have added filters in the app which helps the users to apply according their requirements.

  1. Every musician has a View button so the users can click it without any confusion.
  2. Filters will help users in saving their time while they choose a musician.
  3. Users can check all the available dates of the musician by clicking on available dates button.
  4. Users can directly chat with the musician band and clarify their doubts and specify about their requirements.

LOW-FIDELITY PROTOTYPE

The digital wireframes are modified and low-fi prototypes are designed to make it easy to understand the flow and test.

USABILITY STUDY FINDINGS

We conducted two rounds of unmoderated usability with 5 participants. Findings from the first study helped guide the designs from wireframes to mockups and the second study used the high fidelity prototypes and revealed what aspects of the mockups needed refining.

Round 1 findings

User wants easy navigation without any confusion.

User wants to know the Price details well in advance.

User expects Confirmation before booking.

Round 2 findings

Option to be able to book from available dates page.

Need Filters for recent performance for easy access.

Making the buttons clear.
